Odin Battleship

How to Play

On your turn, click a cell on your opponent's grid to take your shot.

If you sink your opponent's fleet of five ships before your opponent sinks yours, you win!

Ship Placement Rules

Ships can be placed horizontally or vertically, but not diagonally.

Ships must be entirely contained on the board, i.e. no part of the ship can be hanging off the board.

After selecting a ship, press R to rotate it before placing it on the board.
